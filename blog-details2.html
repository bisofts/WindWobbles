Welcome to my home, my life.

  • By Michael Scofield
  • January 14, 2019
  • 8 Comments

I've tried pretty much every cleanser from the PC line. This one and the hydra light are the only 2 that doesn't make my combo/oily skin feel tight and stripped. My husband and I both really love this gentle cleanser. I have acne prone, sensitive, combo/oily, dehydrated, eczema skin. Yes a lot of concerns lol. That's why it's taken years for me to perfect my skin care routine. I have a few high end skin care serums in there but 90% of my routine consist of PC products. After using the PC oil cleansing oil to remove makeup I use this cleanser. It leaves my skin feeling perfectly cleanser without any fighting or over stripping. I will rotate this cleanser and the hydra light depending on my skin that day and/or the season. Nevertheless, this cleanser has never caused breakouts, left any residue on my skin or caused irritation. I believe any skin type can benefit from this cleanser.

Love Skin, Love Myself. Favorite cleanser I ever tried - sensitive skin / rosacea

I'm ordering more Softening Cream Cleanser from Paula's Skin Recovery line. It hasn't dried out my "mature" skin at all . This cleanser is thick and should be liquified in your hand with a few drops of water. I also store the bottle upside down in my cabinet to make dispensing it easier. (The bottle sits well on its top) For travel I transferred some cleanser to a smaller bottle but found it hard to shake out of the smaller bottle. I added a small amount of water and shook to combine the ingredients. Worked perfectly and was much easier to use after that. All in all, I love this product and all those in the Skin Recovery line.

My Mother bought this skin wash for me for Christmas, I have such very dry skin! But not no more, I have been using this wash for 2 and 1/2 weeks and my skin has never been better! I have tried Lovely Skin, the Skin Store looking for something for very dry skin, and if you have dry sensitive skin look no further Paula's choice is for you. Thank You Paula's Choice.
